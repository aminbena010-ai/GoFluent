The Family Picnic

Part 1: Preparation

Every Sunday, my family has a special tradition: we go to the countryside for a picnic. Today is Sunday, and everyone is very busy in the morning. My mother prepares the picnic basket in the kitchen. She puts chicken sandwiches, fresh fruit, and some cookies inside. My father helps her; he makes a large bottle of lemonade with a lot of ice. I look for the picnic blanket and my sister, Emma, finds her ball. We are very excited because the sun is hot and the sky is blue. At 10:30 AM, we put everything in the car and drive to the "Green Valley" park.

Part 2: At the Park

The park is beautiful. There are many tall trees and a small river. When we arrive, my father chooses a place under a big oak tree. It is a perfect spot because there is shade. We spread the blanket on the green grass and sit down. My parents talk and laugh while they eat their sandwiches. Emma and I do not sit for a long time. We run near the river and play with the ball. Our dog, Toby, is with us too. He runs very fast and tries to catch the ball. He is a very happy dog.

Part 3: A Relaxing Afternoon

In the afternoon, we feel a bit tired. My mother reads a book and my father takes a small nap under the tree. I watch the birds in the sky and Emma draws a picture of the river in her notebook. At 4:00 PM, we eat the chocolate cookies. They taste delicious! Then, we collect all our trash because we want to keep the park clean. We say goodbye to the trees and return home in the car. We love our family picnics because we spend time together in nature.
