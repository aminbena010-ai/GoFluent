David's Daily Routine

Part 1: The Morning

My name is David and my daily routine is very organized. I always wake up at 6:45 AM, but I stay in bed for ten minutes. At 6:55 AM, I get up and go to the bathroom. I wash my face and brush my teeth. Then, I get dressed. I usually wear jeans and a comfortable T-shirt. At 7:15 AM, I prepare my breakfast in the kitchen. I eat cereal with milk and I drink a large glass of orange juice. My cat, Luna, sits near me because she wants her food too. After breakfast, I take my backpack and walk to the bus stop.

Part 2: School and Work

I arrive at my office at 8:30 AM. My job is interesting but sometimes it is difficult. I work on my computer and I answer many emails. My boss comes to my desk at 10:00 AM and we talk about new projects. At 1:00 PM, I have lunch with my coworkers. We go to a small Italian restaurant across the street. We eat pasta and talk about our families. It is a very relaxing time. I return to work at 2:00 PM and I finish at 5:00 PM.

Part 3: The Evening

When I get home, I am usually tired. First, I sit on the sofa and read the news on my phone. Then, I cook dinner. I like to cook healthy food, like fish and salad. After dinner, I wash the dishes. In the evening, I exercise for thirty minutes or I watch a series on TV. My sister sometimes calls me and we talk for a long time. Finally, I go to bed at 11:00 PM. I sleep for seven or eight hours every night.
